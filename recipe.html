How it's made
1
Grind cashew
Soak lightly and grind cashew to a fine powder. Quality: no coarse bits
2
Sugar syrup
Cook sugar with water to a 1-string consistency. ⏱ 12 min 🌡 115°C
3
Combine & cook
Mix cashew powder into syrup; cook stirring continuously. ⏱ 8 min
4
Ghee & cardamom
Add ghee and ground cardamom; cook to a soft dough. Quality: leaves pan sides
5
Roll & cut
Roll, cool, and cut into diamonds. ⏱ rest 20 min

Steps

  2. Cook sugar syrup to 1-string (≈115°C).
  3. Mix cashew powder into syrup; cook 8 min.
  4. Add ghee & cardamom; cook to soft dough.
  5. Roll, cool, cut into diamonds.
